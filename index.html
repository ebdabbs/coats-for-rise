Share the Warmth

With our neighbors in need

The Junior League of Greater Princeton is collecting gently used and new Men's, Women's, and Children's Coats for RISE now through December 11th.

What is RISE?

Rise provides social support services in the Hightstown and East Windsor areas. Through community partnerships and direct services, they assist local families and individuals in recovering from setbacks and achieving their full potential. Find out more on their website.

How Can I Help?

We're glad you asked!

  • To donate online, visit our Target Wish List.
  • If you live in the Greater Princeton area, check out our list of coat collection locations.
  • Would you like your business, school, or organization to be included in the coat collection locations? Please fill out this simple form.
  • Finally, spread the word! Download our flyer, Tweet about it, and invite your Facebook friends.
